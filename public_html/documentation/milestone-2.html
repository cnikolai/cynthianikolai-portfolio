Content Strategy

My site will be one page, and will consist of the following sections:

Navigation Bar: The header will be a sticky nav bar banner going across the top of the page that contains links all of the different sections on the page.

Welcome: An extremely brief three word introduction that states my name and the top three words that I used to describe myself. Under the words will be links to my GitHub handle, my LinkedIn profile, and a PDF of my resume.

About Me: In this section, I will write a brief description of myself. The tone of voice will be personable and casual, as my goal is just to give readers a business-casual impression of who I am.

Skills and Computer Proficiencies: This section will highlight my skills and computer proficiencies. I will include the following topics:

  • Programming Languages
  • Front End
  • Back End
  • Databases
  • Visualization/Animation/Design
  • Design Frameworks
  • Geolocation
  • Project Management Collaboration Tools
  • Operating Systems
  • Virtualization
  • Miscellaneous

Portfolio: This section will be an animated, responsive image gallery containing 4-6 examples of my best work for each category. Categories included are Front End, Full Stack, Android, iOS, and Animations. When the user clicks on a category, this section will display/change the gallery shown based on the category that was selected.

Fun Fact: This section will feature a fun fact about myself. I am building the world's largest jigsaw puzzle, and I will have a brief sentence about it under which is a carousel that contains 4 pictures of the puzzle.

Contact Me: This section will contain a simple contact form, with a spot for a sender to include his/her email address and a short message.

Footer: The footer will be a banner going across the bottom of the page that contains links to my twitter and facebook accounts.

Wireframes

This site will feature one layout that will be fully responsive on small and large screens. These wireframes were created using Sketch.
